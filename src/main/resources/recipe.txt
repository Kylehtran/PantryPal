

Title: Steak with Garlic-Butter Mushrooms

Ingredients:
- Steak
- Garlic
- Butter
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper

#1 Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
#2 Place steak on a baking sheet.
#3 Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper.
#4 Bake steak in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
#5 Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
#6 Add mushrooms and garlic to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are golden brown, about 5 minutes.
#7 Remove steak from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
#8 Serve steak with garlic-butter mushrooms.
