

Title: Chicken Lunch

Ingredients: 
- Chicken
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Onion
- Carrot
- Celery

Instructions:
# Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil.
# Add the chicken to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
# Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.
# Add the garlic, onion, carrot, and celery to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
# Serve the chicken and vegetables with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
