

Title: Raspberry Dinner

Ingredients: 
- Raspberry 
- Olive oil 
- Salt 
- Pepper 
- Garlic 
- Onion 
- Chicken 
- Rice

#1 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
#2 Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
#3 Add garlic, onion, salt, and pepper to the skillet and cook until the onion is translucent.
#4 Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until the chicken is cooked through.
#5 Grease a baking dish with olive oil.
#6 Place the chicken in the baking dish.
#7 Top the chicken with the raspberry.
#8 Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
#9 While the chicken is baking, cook the rice according to package instructions.
#10 Serve the chicken and raspberry over the cooked rice. Enjoy!
