

Title: Bacon and Egg Breakfast

Ingredients: 
- Bacon 
- Egg 
- Salt 
- Pepper 
- Butter 
- Toast

#1 Heat a skillet over medium heat. 
#2 Add bacon to the skillet and cook until desired crispness. 
#3 Remove bacon from skillet and set aside. 
#4 Crack the egg into the skillet. 
#5 Sprinkle with salt and pepper. 
#6 Cook until desired doneness. 
#7 Place a pat of butter onto each slice of toast. 
#8 Place the cooked egg onto one slice of toast and the bacon onto the other. 
#9 Serve and enjoy!
