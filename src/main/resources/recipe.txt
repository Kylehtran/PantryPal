

Title: Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo 

Ingredients: 
- Chicken Breast 
- Broccoli 
- Alfredo Sauce 
- Butter 
- Garlic 
- Salt 
- Pepper 
- Olive Oil 
- Pasta 

#1 Heat a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. 
#2 Add pasta to boiling water and cook until al dente. 
#3 Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
#4 Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. 
#5 Cook chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 8 minutes. 
#6 Remove chicken from skillet and set aside. 
#7 Add butter and garlic to skillet and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. 
#8 Add broccoli to skillet and season with salt and pepper. 
#9 Cook broccoli until tender, about 5 minutes. 
#10 Add Alfredo sauce and cooked chicken to skillet and stir to combine. 
#11 Reduce heat to low and simmer until sauce is heated through, about 5 minutes. 
#12 Drain cooked pasta and add to skillet with chicken and broccoli. 
#13 Stir to combine and season with salt and pepper, to taste. 
#14 Serve chicken and broccoli Alfredo immediately.
