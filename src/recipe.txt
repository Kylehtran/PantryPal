

Title: Grilled Chicken 
- Chicken 
- Olive Oil 
- Salt 
- Pepper 
- Garlic Powder 

#1 Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. 
#2 Rub the chicken with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. 
#3 Place the chicken on the preheated grill.
#4 Grill the chicken for 5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees Fahrenheit. 
#5 Remove the chicken from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
