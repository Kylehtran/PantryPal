

Title: Beef and Cheese Tortilla Wrap

Ingredients:
- Tortilla
- Beef
- Cheese
- Oil
- Salt

#1 Heat a pan over medium heat and add oil.
#2 Add beef to pan and season with salt.
#3 Cook beef until lightly browned.
#4 Place the tortilla on a plate.
#5 Spread cheese over the tortilla.
#6 Place cooked beef over the cheese.
#7 Fold the tortilla in half.
#8 Heat a pan over medium heat.
#9 Place the folded tortilla in the pan and cook until golden brown on both sides.
#10 Serve warm.
