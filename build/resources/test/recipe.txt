

Title: Sushi Breakfast 
Ingredients: 
- Sushi Rice 
- Nori
- Sushi Vinegar
- Sushi Fillings (such as salmon, tuna, avocado, cucumber, etc.)

#1 Cook the sushi rice according to package instructions.
#2 Mix the sushi vinegar with the cooked sushi rice.
#3 Cut the nori into small pieces.
#4 Place a sheet of nori on a sushi mat.
#5 Spread the sushi rice over the nori.
#6 Place the sushi fillings over the sushi rice.
#7 Roll the sushi mat up, pressing firmly to form a log shape.
#8 Cut the log into individual pieces.
#9 Serve and enjoy!.......
