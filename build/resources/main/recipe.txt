

Title: Pancakes 
Ingredients: 
- Flour 
- Milk 
- Eggs 
- Baking powder 
- Salt 
- Butter 
- Maple syrup

#1 In a medium bowl, whisk together 1 cup of flour, 1 cup of milk, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. 
#2 Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat. 
#3 Pour the batter into the skillet and cook until bubbles form on the top. 
#4 Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown. 
#5 Repeat steps 2-4 for the remaining batter. 
#6 Serve the pancakes with butter and maple syrup.....
