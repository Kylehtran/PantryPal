

Title: Kevin's Breakfast 
Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Milk

#1 Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
#2 Spread butter evenly over both slices of bread.
#3 Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them together with a fork.
#4 Add a splash of milk to the eggs and mix together.
#5 Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook until the eggs are set.
#6 Place the slices of bread in the toaster and toast to desired darkness.
#7 Place the eggs on top of one of the slices of bread.
#8 Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
#9 Place the other slice of bread on top and enjoy!...........................................................
