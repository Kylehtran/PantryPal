

Title: Sausage and Egg Breakfast 
Ingredients:
- Egg 
- Sausage 
- Oil 
- Salt 
- Pepper 

#1 Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
#2 Add sausage and cook until browned and cooked through.
#3 Remove sausage from skillet and set aside.
#4 In the same skillet, add the egg and season with salt and pepper.
#5 Cook egg until desired doneness.
#6 Serve egg and sausage together.
