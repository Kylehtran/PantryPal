

Title: Kevin's Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 cup of white rice
- 1 onion
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 teaspoon of paprika
- 1 teaspoon of oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

#1 Preheat oven to 375F.
#2 Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
#3 Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
#4 Add the chicken to the skillet and season with paprika, oregano, salt and pepper.
#5 Cook the chicken for about 8 minutes, flipping once halfway through.
#6 Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
#7 Add the rice to the skillet and stir to combine.
#8 Add 1 cup of water and bring to a boil.
#9 Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
#10 Place the chicken on top of the cooked rice and transfer the skillet to the preheated oven.
#11 Bake for 15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
#12 Serve and enjoy!.........................................
