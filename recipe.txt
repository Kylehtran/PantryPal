

Title: Beef and Rice 

Ingredients: 
- Beef 
- Rice 
- Onion 
- Garlic 
- Olive oil 
- Salt 
- Pepper 

#1 Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. 
#2 Add olive oil and diced onion and garlic and sauté until softened. 
#3 Add the beef and cook until browned. 
#4 Add the rice, salt, and pepper and stir to combine. 
#5 Reduce heat to low and cover the pan. 
#6 Cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
#7 Serve and enjoy!
