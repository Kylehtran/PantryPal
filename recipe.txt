

Title: Balut and Chicken Stir-Fry 

Ingredients: 
- Chicken 
- Balut 
- Carrots
- Soy sauce
- Garlic
- Cooking oil

#1 Heat up a pan with oil over medium heat.

#2 Add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.

#3 Add chicken and balut and stir-fry for about 3 minutes.

#4 Add carrots and stir-fry for about 2 minutes.

#5 Add soy sauce and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.

#6 Serve hot.
