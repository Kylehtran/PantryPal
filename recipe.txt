

Title: Chicken Balut Carrots

Ingredients:
- Chicken
- Balut
- Carrots
- Onion
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper

#1 Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil. 
#2 Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
#3 Add the chicken and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
#4 Add the balut and carrots and cook until the carrots are tender, about 7 minutes.
#5 Season with salt and pepper to taste.
#6 Serve warm.
