

Title: Orange Wraith

Ingredients:
- Wraith
- Oranges
- Sugar
- Salt
- Butter

#1 Preheat oven to 350°F.

#2 Peel and slice oranges into thin wedges.

#3 Place the orange slices in a bowl and sprinkle with sugar and salt.

#4 In a separate bowl, mix together the butter and wraith until it forms a paste.

#5 Spread the wraith mixture over the orange slices.

#6 Place the orange slices on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes.

#7 Remove from the oven and let cool before serving.
