

Title: Chocolate Chip Banana Bread 
Ingredients:
- All-purpose flour
- Baking powder 
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Bananas
- Granulated sugar
- Light brown sugar
- Unsalted butter
- Egg
- Vanilla extract
- Semi-sweet chocolate chips

#1 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan with parchment paper.

#2 In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

#3 In a large bowl, mash the bananas. Add the granulated sugar, light brown sugar, butter, egg, and vanilla extract. Whisk until combined and smooth.

#4 Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.

#5 Fold in the chocolate chips.

#6 Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

#7 Let the banana bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
