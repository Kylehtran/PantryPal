

Title: Tomato and Zucchini Casserole
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 2 zucchini, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

#1 Preheat oven to 350°F.

#2 In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, zucchini, onion, garlic, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper.

#3 Mix until all ingredients are evenly distributed.

#4 Transfer mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.

#5 Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.

#6 Serve hot. Enjoy!
