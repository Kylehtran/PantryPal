

Title: Balut and Chicken Stir-Fry 

Ingredients: 
- Chicken 
- Balut 
- Carrots 
- Soy sauce 
- Garlic 
- Onion 
- Vegetable oil 

#1 Heat a large pan over medium heat. 
#2 Add vegetable oil to the pan. 
#3 Add garlic and onion to the pan and saut√© until fragrant. 
#4 Add chicken and balut to the pan and cook until chicken is no longer pink. 
#5 Add carrots to the pan and cook for 3 minutes. 
#6 Add soy sauce to the pan and stir to combine. 
#7 Cook for an additional 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
#8 Serve hot.
