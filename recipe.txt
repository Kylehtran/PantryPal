

Title: Chicken Balut Carrot Stir-Fry

Ingredients: 
- Chicken
- Balut
- Carrots
- Cooking oil
- Soy sauce
- Salt
- Pepper

#1 Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat and add cooking oil.
#2 Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes or until lightly browned.
#3 Add balut and cook for 3 minutes.
#4 Add carrots and cook for 2 minutes.
#5 Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
#6 Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until everything is cooked through.
#7 Serve hot.
