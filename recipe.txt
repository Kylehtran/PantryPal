

Title: Ham and Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients: 
- Rice
- Eggs
- Ham
- Carrots
- Oil
- Soy sauce
- Salt

#1 Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
#2 Add ham and carrots and cook until vegetables are tender.
#3 Push the ham and carrots to one side of the pan and add eggs.
#4 Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through.
#5 Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
#6 Add soy sauce and salt to taste.
#7 Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
#8 Serve.
