

Title: Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients: 
- Beef
- Olive oil
- Onion
- Garlic
- Carrot
- Broccoli
- Soy sauce

#1 Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil.
#2 Add the beef and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
#3 Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and broccoli and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
#4 Add the soy sauce and cook for another minute.
#5 Serve the stir fry over rice or noodles.
