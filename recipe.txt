

Title: Balut and Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients: 
- Chicken 
- Balut 
- Carrots 
- Soy Sauce 
- Garlic 
- Olive Oil 
- Salt 
- Pepper 

#1 Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

#2 Add olive oil and garlic to the pan.

#3 Cook the garlic for 1-2 minutes, stirring often.

#4 Add the chicken and season with salt and pepper.

#5 Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring often, until chicken is cooked through.

#6 Add the balut and carrots to the pan.

#7 Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring often.

#8 Add soy sauce and stir to combine.

#9 Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

#10 Serve hot. Enjoy!
