

Title: Fruit Salad

Ingredients:
- Strawberries
- Apples
- Oranges
- Lemon juice
- Honey

#1 Cut the strawberries, apples, and oranges into small pieces.

#2 Place the cut fruit in a bowl.

#3 Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the bowl of fruit.

#4 Drizzle honey over the fruit.

#5 Mix the fruit together until evenly coated.

#6 Serve and enjoy!
