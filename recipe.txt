

Title: Cheesy Potato Casserole 
- Potatoes 
- Onion 
- Milk 
- Butter 
- Salt 
- Pepper 
- Cheese 

#1 Preheat oven to 375Â°F. 
#2 Peel and chop potatoes into cubes. 
#3 Chop onion. 
#4 In a saucepan, melt butter and add potatoes and onion. 
#5 Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
#6 Add milk, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes. 
#7 Grease a baking dish with butter. 
#8 Pour potato mixture into dish. 
#9 Top with cheese. 
#10 Bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and potatoes are cooked through. 
#11 Serve and enjoy!
