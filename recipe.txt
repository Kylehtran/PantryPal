

Title: Chicken Balut Stir Fry

- Chicken
- Balut
- Carrots
- Soy Sauce
- Oyster Sauce
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Sesame Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

Step 1: Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Add sesame oil to the wok or skillet and heat until shimmering.

Step 3: Add the chicken and balut and cook until lightly browned, stirring often.

Step 4: Add the carrots, garlic, and ginger and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring often.

Step 5: Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper and stir to combine.

Step 6: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 7: Serve and enjoy!
